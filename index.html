About G3 Golf Sims

Bring the country club feel home.

We help golfers create a simulator setup that feels intentional, polished, and built to perform. Every project is planned around your space, your goals, and the way you want to play.

What we focus on

Whether you want a clean practice setup, a full entertainment space, or a premium indoor golf experience, G3 Golf Sims builds around your needs instead of forcing you into a one-size-fits-all package.

  • Custom layouts for garages, homes, barns, and commercial spaces

  • Budget-conscious options without sacrificing a clean finished look
  • Setup designed for practice, entertainment, and year-round golf
